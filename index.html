Lingocap: A brief overview

  • 1

    The Problem

    The big video-communication giants Zoom, Google Meets and Skype do not offer live speech captioning for Hindi language and other Indian dialects. A infrastructure gap that makes it impossible for people with hearing impairments, speaking Indian dialects, to communicate with family and friends without embarrasement.

  • 2

    The Solution

    Lingocap solves for inclusion and accesibility of people with hearing and speech impairment by providing Indian language live captioning in 5 Indian languages and ISL translation. Thereby making communication and socialising accessible and inclusive for more than 75L+ deaf and speech impaired people of India.

FAQ

Basics

How do I add captions in session? + −

  • Start your session
  • on the bottom left you'll see a blue plus (+) sign, click on it to open the opens menu
  • Click on 'Write Close Captions'
  • Select your language
  • Select the user you would like to write the captions, and change their role to 'Closed Captionist'

Can I use Lingocap on multiple devices? + −

  • You can be signed in to Lingocap on one computer, one tablet, and one phone at a time. If you sign into an additional device while logged into another device of the same type, you will not be logged out on the first device.

How do I record a Lingocap session? + −

To start recording

  • The recording button is located in the top of Lingocap interface, to the right of the session title.
  • To start the recording, select the start recording button.
  • After a pop up window pops up, click Yes
  • When started, the recording icon will change to red and a timer will begin counting the total number of recorded minutes captured.

To pause recording

  • To stop or pause the recording, select the red icon and timer which will be in the place of start recording button
  • To start the recording, select the start recording button.
  • After a pop up window pops up, click Yes
  • After clicking yes, your recording will be paused.

To resume recording

  • Click the Resume recording in the same place
  • After a pop up window pops up, click Yes
  • The session will be recorded again.

How do I Sign Up and change password? + −

To Sign Up:

  • Click on Sign Up button on the top right corner of your screen
  • By clicking on the Sign up button, you will be directed to the sign up page and will be required to fill your details.
  • Once you sign up, you will have access to use Lingocap

To change password:

  • Click on password
  • Once you go to the password page, you will be required to type your old password to have a new password.
  • Press update once you have changed your password.
